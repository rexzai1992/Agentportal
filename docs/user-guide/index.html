Travel Agent Portal — User Guide

A simple step-by-step guide for everyone: Agents, Partners, Admin, Finance, and Gate Staff — from registration all the way to redeeming the voucher QR at the gate. Every screenshot is a real screen from the portal.

2. Agent & Partner — How to Register

New companies apply from the login page. No account is needed to register.

Agent or Partner?
Agent → must upload KPL Form + SSM Form  •  Partner → only needs the SSM Form

1Open the portal

Go to the login page. You will see three buttons: Become an Agent, Become a Partner, and Check Application Status.

2Accept the Terms and Conditions

Click Become an Agent (or Become a Partner). Read the terms, prepare your documents, then click Accept.

3Fill in your Company Information

Enter your company name, registration number, email, KPL license (agents only), contact number, and address. Click Save and Next.

4Upload the Required Documents

Drag and drop your KPL Form and SSM Form (JPG, PNG, or PDF, max 5 MB). A green tick appears when the upload succeeds.

5Add your Contact Person

Fill in the name, email, and phone number. You can add up to 3 contact persons.

6Company Background & Submit

Target Market and Sales Channel are optional. Tick the Privacy Policy & Terms box, then click Submit.

7Save your Application ID

A success screen shows your Application ID. Write it down — you need it to check your status. A confirmation email is also sent to you.

3. Check Application Status

Anytime after applying, check where your application stands.

On the login page click Check Application Status and enter your Application ID.

StatusMeaning
PENDINGUnder review — no action needed
APPROVEDCheck your email for your username + temporary password
REVISIONSomething needs fixing — check the email remarks
REJECTEDApplication declined — check your email

Forgot your password?

Click Forgot password? on the login page, enter your username + email, and a temporary password will be emailed to you.

4. Admin — Approving Registrations

Admin reviews the documents and creates the account with one click.

Login as Admin. The dashboard shows pending agent/partner requests, totals, and pending payment approvals — click any card to jump to that page.

1Open the request queue

Go to Agent Requests (or Partner Requests) in the sidebar. Search and filter by status.

2Review the application

Click Review. You can see all company details, open the uploaded KPL/SSM documents, and the contact persons.

3Approve, Reject, or ask for Revision

  • Approve — no remarks needed
  • Rejected / Revision — remarks are required (the applicant receives them by email)

When you click Approve, the system automatically creates the account (code like A20260002) and emails the applicant their username + temporary password (valid 24 hours).

4Manage the account

The new company appears in Active Agents / Active Partners.

Click View to open the account — change the account status or expiry date here.

5. Admin — Purchase Schemes

Agents can only buy products inside a scheme that is bound to their account. Do this once per account.

1Create a Purchase Scheme

Go to Purchase Schemes → New Purchase Scheme. Give it a code + name, pick an effective date, click Add Product, and set the price, min/max quantity, and incentive % per product. Click Save.

Click View on a scheme to change its status (Active/Inactive) or add a new revision (new prices starting from a new effective date).

2Bind the scheme to an account

Open the account (Active Agents → View) → View / Bind Scheme → Add New Revision → choose the effective date, the scheme, and an optional incentive. Use the Standard or Special tab.

Important: if an agent sees “No products are available”, it means no scheme is bound to their account yet.

6. Admin — Announcements

Share notices with agents and partners.

Go to Announcements → Add New Announcement:

  • Display Type = Home Page → shows as a popup + list on agent/partner dashboards (choose the audience: Agent / Partner / both)
  • Display Type = Login Page → shows on the login page
  • Set effective + expiry dates, optionally attach a JPG/PDF, set status Active

7. Agent & Partner — First Login

Use the account code and temporary password from your approval email.

1Login and change your password

Login with your account code (e.g. A20260002) and the temporary password. You will be asked to change your password first — minimum 5 characters with an uppercase letter, lowercase letter, number, and special character.

2Your dashboard

Home announcements pop up when you land on your dashboard.

The dashboard shows your Pending Approvals, Incomplete Orders, and Today's Purchases.

8. Agent & Partner — Buying Tickets

Pick products from your scheme, build a cart, and submit.

1Ticket Purchase

Open Ticket Management → Ticket Purchase. Pick the product type, enter a quantity (the min/max per item is shown), and click Add. Your cart appears in the Order Summary. Click Submit.

2Transaction Review

You are taken to the Transaction Review: reference number, status, line items, totals, and the bank information for your transfer.

9. Agent & Partner — Pay Offline

After making the bank transfer, upload your proof of payment.

1Click Pay Offline

  1. Upload your proof of payment (bank slip — drag & drop)
  2. Choose the Payment Group and Payment Type
  3. Click Submit

Your order becomes PENDING APPROVAL — Finance and Admin take over next.

Didn't finish paying? — Incomplete Orders

Orders you created but haven't paid are listed in Incomplete Order. Click Complete to go back and pay.

10. Finance — Mark Payment Paid

Finance verifies the money really arrived before Admin can approve.

Login as Finance. You land on the Offline Payments list.

1Open the payment and check the slip

Click the row. Check the purchase details and open the uploaded bank slip to verify the transfer.

2Mark Payment Paid

If the transfer is genuine, click Mark Payment Paid. This does not generate tickets — it only confirms the money is verified.

Rule: Admin cannot approve a payment before Finance marks it paid.

11. Admin — Approve the Payment (vouchers are created here)

The final approval that generates the vouchers.

1Open pending payments

2Review and decide

Open the payment. You can see the purchase details, the slip, and when Finance marked it paid. In Payment Approval choose:

  • Approved → the order is confirmed and vouchers are automatically generated (one voucher per ticket, each with its own serial number + QR)
  • Rejected / Revision → a reason is required; the agent receives an email

12. Agent & Partner — Vouchers & QR

Once the payment is approved, your vouchers are ready.

Open Ticket Management → Voucher Issued. Each row is one order: how many vouchers were issued, used, and still available.

Click View QR to see every voucher: serial number, dates, gate, redeem status (NEW LOCKED REDEEMED), and a View QR button that displays the QR code for that voucher — show this QR at the gate.

Reports

Reports lists all your purchases and payments. Click Export To Excel to download an .xlsx file.

13. Agent & Partner — Profile & Account Renewal

View your company info and renew before your account expires.

Profile shows your company information, documents, and account expiry. When your account is within 2 months of expiry, a Request Renewal button appears — upload your new KPL/SSM, enter the license number + expiry date, and submit.

If your account expires: you can still log in, but Ticket Purchase is hidden and blocked until Admin approves your renewal.

14. Admin — Complimentary (free tickets)

Give free vouchers to an agent or partner.

Go to Ticket Management → Complimentary Voucher: select the agent/partner, click Add Product, set quantities (minimum 20 tickets total), and Submit. Free vouchers are issued immediately at RM 0.

15. Admin — Account Renewals

Approve renewal requests to extend account expiry.

Renewal requests appear under Renewals. Click Review and choose Approve / Reject / Revision (remarks required for reject/revision). On approval, the account expiry is extended and the agent is emailed.

16. Admin — Reports

Filter by date and export to Excel.

Open the Report menu in the sidebar. It contains nine reports:

  • Transaction Report / Transaction Details — all portal transactions (order level / line-item level)
  • Purchase Summary / Purchase Details — purchases with payment and approval info
  • Top Purchase — most purchased products
  • Voucher Issued — issued / used / available vouchers
  • Ticket — search a ticket by serial no or QR code
  • Complimentary and Payment

Each report has Filter By User, Filter By Company, and date filters, a quick Search box, Show 15/30/50/100 rows with Previous/Next paging, a TOTAL row for amounts, and Export To Excel.

17. Staff — Redeeming the Voucher QR at the Gate

The final step: the customer arrives at the gate with the voucher.

1Open Voucher Redeem

Login as Staff and open Voucher Redeem in the sidebar.

2Scan or type the code

Scan the QR code (or type the serial number, e.g. APM-V-26-134875_012), optionally enter the gate name, and click Redeem Voucher.

The system rejects the voucher if it is:

  • ❌ already redeemed (each voucher works only once)
  • ❌ expired or not yet effective
  • ❌ locked
  • ❌ not found

The agent's voucher list updates instantly — the Used count goes up and that serial becomes REDEEMED.
